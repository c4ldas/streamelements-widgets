<h2 id="widget-name" class="widget-name">Snow Widget</h2>

<p id="description" class="description">
Add a customisable snowfall effect to your overlay. Control the number of snowflakes, their size, falling speed, wind, and fade behaviour to create a dynamic winter atmosphere on your stream. You can also enable or disable it via a chat command.
</p>

<p>
This widget is visually similar to the native StreamElements widget <code>Let it snow</code>, but offers more configuration options.
</p>

<h2>Preview and Instructions</h2>

<h3>How to use</h3>
<p>1. After installing the widget, use the available sliders to adjust snowflake size, speed, amount, and wind.</p>
<p>2. You can control the fade-in and fade-out effects of the snow, or disable them entirely.</p>
<p>3. The option <code>Start from the top</code> defines whether the snow starts falling from the top of the screen or simply appears when the overlay loads.</p>
<p>4. You can configure a chat command to enable or disable the widget.</p>
<p>5. Commands can be restricted to mods, VIPs, subs, or everyone using the dropdown menu.</p>
